Our Services

Farmer

Sell your fresh produce directly to restaurants and food stall owners without middlemen. Set your prices, receive bulk or small orders, and get fair payments instantly. Our platform ensures you reach the right buyers while maximizing your profits.

Restaurant

Source farm-fresh ingredients directly from verified farmers at competitive prices. Place bulk orders, set your budget, and negotiate directly to get the best deals. Enjoy a streamlined procurement process with real-time tracking and secure payments.

Individual

Buy fresh produce in smaller quantities at fair rates, just like big restaurants. Get access to high-quality ingredients directly from farmers, ensuring fresh and affordable supplies for your daily business needs, also you can buy goods everyday as per you need.
